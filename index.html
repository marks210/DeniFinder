How DeniFinder Works

Finding your perfect home in Africa has never been easier. Here's how we make it happen in just 3 simple steps.

Search Available Spots

Browse through our verified listings with real-time availability status. No more disappointment from outdated posts.

View Map & Details

Explore properties on our interactive map, view photos, amenities, and get all the details you need to make an informed decision.

Chat or Use Advance Assist

Message landlords directly through our secure platform or let our Advance Assist service find and book your perfect home.

Book Help Before You Move

Planning to relocate? Our Advance Assist service ensures you never miss the perfect home. Get early access to new listings and personalized support.

  • Early access to new listings
  • Personalized alerts for your preferences
  • Dedicated support team
  • Schedule viewings in advance
  • Verified properties only

Unique & Reliable Extra Services

Property Verification Service

Send field agents to verify the authenticity of property ownership, location, and condition.
Helps clients avoid scams.

Legal Assistance & Land Title Processing

Partner with lawyers to help clients process land papers, sales agreements, lease contracts, etc.

Secure Payment & Deposit Holding

Offer a secure platform where you temporarily hold deposits until property handover is done (escrow-like service).

Tenant Background Checks

Help landlords vet potential tenants for reliability (especially in areas like student hostels).

"Rent Now, Pay Monthly" Option

Partner with microfinance services to allow monthly payment plans (especially for students or young workers).

Moving & Transport Services

Help clients move their belongings easily especially students or people changing houses.

Furniture & Appliance Rentals

Offer temporary rentals of beds, stoves, fridges for students or new tenants.

Cleaning & Maintenance Services

Deep cleaning before move-in, pest control, plumbing, or painting touch-ups.

Internet & Utility Setup

Help clients set up internet, water, and electricity quickly when they move in.

Area Reviews & Cost of Living Info

Share reviews of neighborhoods, safety levels, transport options, nearby shops, and average prices.
